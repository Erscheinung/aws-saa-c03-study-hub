Deduction & Exam Tips

01
Eliminate the Obvious
If an option mentions a service that doesn't exist or has wrong specs (e.g., "Lambda 30-minute timeout"), eliminate it immediately.
02
Watch for "Least Effort"
AWS loves managed services. If the question asks for "least operational overhead," prefer serverless/managed options.
03
Cost Keywords
"Cost-effective" often means Spot Instances, S3 Intelligent-Tiering, or Reserved capacity. Don't over-engineer.
04
Security First
30% of the exam! Remember: IAM policies attach to principals, resource policies attach to resources. Encryption = KMS.
05
Read ALL Options
Don't jump to the first "correct" answer. There may be a MORE correct one. Look for the "most" appropriate.
06
Flag & Move On
Stuck for 60+ seconds? Flag it and move on. Your brain will work on it subconsciously. Come back later.
